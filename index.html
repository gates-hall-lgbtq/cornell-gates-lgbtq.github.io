LGBTQ+ Students in Computing and Information Science


About us

We are a group of LGBTQ+ and ally graduate students in Computing and Information Science at Cornell. We work with Cornell faculty and staff to keep the department safe and friendly, and to provide for the needs of LGBTQ+ students.

What we do

LGBTea, lunch with LGBTQ faculty, slack group, facebook group.

What we've done

Negotiated for an All-Gender Restroom in Gates Hall. Acquired pronoun stickers for Information Science Ph.D. Visit Day. Give a welcoming talk at IS Ph.D. visit day?

What we are working on

LGBT trainings for Computing and Information science faculty and staff, especially with respect to Trans issues and rights. Providing "Safe Space" stickers for faculty to use, should they wish to.
